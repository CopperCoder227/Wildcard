A game developed and designed by Toby Fox.
Deltarune is an episodic role-playing video game developed by Toby Fox as a follow-up to his 2015 video game Undertale. The game features new characters, music, and adventures, and expands on the systems in Undertale, such as adding Party Members, a magic system, and the drowsy system. The story follows a human named Kris, who falls into a mysterious world of darkness and is prompted by its inhabitants to seal Dark Fountains powering the Dark Worlds to prevent a cataclysm.
